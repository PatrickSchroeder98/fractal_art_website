"Inspiration"

Artist: Patrick Schröder

Year: 2024

Medium: Poster paper 200; printer: Eco Solvent Mutoh

Size: 124,5 cm x 89 cm


This painting represents an idea forming in the mind. The viewer can see the clarified idea in the center, as well as a shadow in the background representing the idea that has not fully formed. Both the idea and the shadow share the same shape, symbolizing the connection between these two elements. Some parts of the idea are visible behind a fog, representing the need for learning and exploration while being inspired.

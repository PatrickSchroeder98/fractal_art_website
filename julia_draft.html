Julia Set Drafts

Artist: Patrick Schröder

Year: 2022 - 2024

Medium: Digital files

Size: Various resolutions of images


The Julia set is the second fractal that I'm exploring. Similar to the Mandelbrot set, I'm using various zoom levels and coloring functions. I also include a self-discovered modification in the equation that makes the lines in the background much more visible.

Spring Flowers Draft

Artist: Patrick Schröder

Year: 2024

Medium: Poster paper

Size: 32,5 cm x 40,5 cm


This draft was created using a technique that merges fractals with photos. It creates a unique blend of realism and abstraction, playing with shapes and colors. Printed and framed, this painting is a testament to the various possibilities that the Python programming language can offer in the field of digital art.
